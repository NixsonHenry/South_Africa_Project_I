What is the South Africa?

South Africa is one of the countries on the African continent renowned for its varied topography, great natural beauty, and cultural diversity, all of which have made the country a favoured destination for travelers. South Africa is made up of nine cities and seventy six towns: Eastern Cape with its 11 towns, Free State with its 10 towns, Gauteng with its 15 towns, Kwazulu-Natal including its 8 towns, Limpopo also including its 8 towns, Mpumalanga with its 3 towns, North West with its 5 towns, the two last one are Northern Cape including its 5 towns and Western Cape and its 11 towns.

These featured cities are home to delightfully-diverse restaurants, charming town and places. South Africa, at the tip of the African continent has so much to offer to everyone, is known as the adventure capital of the world. It has almost 3000 km of beautiful coastline.
