About Us

Haiti Tourism is a Tour Guides Haitian company. We provide information to South African Tourists and offer services like: take tourists around a city and show them the main landmarks and places they are visiting, and answer questions they may have.



Our Tour Guides are knowledgeable about the places they show to tourists. On tours that include sightseeing historical buildings, sites, and locations, our Tour Guides are also able to provide travelers with all the information they should know about the place they’re visiting. OurTour Guides work on a pre-established route and visit a selected list of locations to avoid they’re getting lost. We also are responsible to make better places for our tourists to live in and visit, provides enjoyable and unforgettable experiences for the tourists. Overall, we want to ensure the well-being of our host communities.
