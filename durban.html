TRY SHARK CAGE DIVING IN GANSBAAI

Ever wanted to get up close and personal with one of the most feared predators on earth? Gansbaai is roughly a two and a half hour drive from Cape Town and has one of the highest concentrations of Great White sharks in the world.

ZIP-LINING OVER THE KOEKEDOUW RIVER IN CERES

Ceres is located in the heart of the Western Cape and is one of the country’s most picturesque towns with rocky mountains and citrus farms all around.
